Privacy Policy for Grow.io

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Summary: Grow.io does not collect, store, or share personal information. Game data is stored locally on your device only.

This Privacy Policy applies to Grow.io, published on the App Store as “Grow.io: Swarm Clash.”

1. Data Collection

Grow.io does not collect, store, or transmit personal information. Game data, such as scores, statistics, and progress, is stored locally on your device using platform-provided local storage.

2. No Third-Party Services

At this time, Grow.io does not use third-party services such as:

  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media integrations

3. No Internet Required

Grow.io currently works offline and does not require an internet connection. The app does not transmit personal information over the network.

4. Children's Privacy

Grow.io does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any users.

5. Data Storage

Game progress and statistics are stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not uploaded to a server by the app
  • Can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling

6. Permissions

Grow.io does not request special device permissions at this time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If the app's data practices change, for example due to online features, analytics, or advertising, this page will be updated with a new revision date.

8. Your Rights

Because Grow.io does not collect personal information, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. Game data remains under your control on your device.
